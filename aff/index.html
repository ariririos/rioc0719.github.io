What We Are

The Academic Framework Forum (AFF) is a new organization that intends to promote academic success for everyone at OHS. We're a place where people can come to discuss new ideas about academic support, get help organizing those ideas, and support others' ideas and projects.

The Examinations and Assessments Project

We run SAT/AP/SAT II practice tests on Saturday mornings at the County Library, and address issues with availability of these tests by providing fee waivers for printing and transportation assistance. Qualified student proctors oversee the test and provide their advice once it's done.
