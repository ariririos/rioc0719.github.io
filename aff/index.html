A Framework Forward

What is the Academic Framework Forum?

The Academic Framework Forum (AFF) is a new organization that intends to promote academic success for everyone at OHS. We are a place where people can come to discuss new ideas about academic support, get help organizing those ideas, and support others' ideas and projects.

What does the AFF do?

Our main project is the Academic Support Network, which runs a network of Teaching Assistants in many classes on campus. These Teaching Assistants answer questions, do one-on-one tutoring, run study groups, have review sessions, and more.

We also run SAT/AP/SAT II practice tests, and soon plan to assist with the college applications process and connect students with summer programs at MIT, UF, and other colleges.
