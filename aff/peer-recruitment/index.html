the AFF
Become an AFF peer tutor and change OHS for the better.
We're OHS students helping other students succeed through peer tutoring. We're the Academic Framework Forum.

We're recruiting for tutors in every subject, at every level: from Liberal Arts Math to Algebra I to AP Calculus AB; from Leadership to English I Honors to AP Lit; from SAT to ACT to PSAT. We want every OHS student to have access to a tutor and we're asking our fellow students for help.

What you can help with:
  • Math
  • Science
  • English
  • Social studies
  • Translating Spanish to English
  • Standardized exams (SAT, ACT, etc.)
  • Mentoring other students about college and career
Never tutored before? Wondering what you'll get out of it?

No worries! We'll teach you how to tutor and mentor, and give you the resources you'll need to succeed: transportation, funding, location, and logistics.

Not only can you get community service hours from this, you'll get support with writing your college and scholarship applications from Cristian Rios, MIT student and 2018 OHS graduate.

Other incentives (including $$ and special college/career visits) are in the works!

We want students who are committed to helping others, no matter if you think you're a good tutor or mentor.

We're students helping other students succeed by filling in gaps where OHS is lacking: including exam preparation and tutoring that works with students' schedules.

We're a fully student-run organization. Don't want to tutor? You can help in other ways, like fundraising, outreach, and recruitment.

Join us! We're making OHS a better place for all students!
