the PLUS ULTRA INITATIVE

Proposal Intro

The Plus Ultra Initiative is a project of the Academic Framework Forum, an organization of 22 student volunteers with a mission of promoting academic success for everyone at OHS.

Our work consists of placing Teaching Assistants in various classes, running SAT practice tests, and starting outreach efforts for summer programs; the Plus Ultra Initiative is special in that it targets a much smaller subset of the OHS student body.

Plus Ultra targets the most talented and ambitious students in the Classses of 2021 and 2020 for a multi-year mentoring relationship with Class of 2018 students.

The Academic Framework Forum

The Plus Ultra Initiative is backed by the mission of the Academic Framework Forum:

The Academic Framework Forum intends to create a lasting academic support network to ensure academic success for all students at Okeechobee High School across a variety of subjects through an open forum to discuss, debate, develop, and implement new ideas and approaches to promoting scholastic achievement.
